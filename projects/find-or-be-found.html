Find or be Found

Overview

Find or Be Found is my debut premium game, both as a game developer and as a co-founder of Studio Downstairs. As the sole programmer, I led the planning and execution of the large-scale project, which proved both challenging and highly rewarding. I gained hands-on experience across the full development cycle, from prototyping to release and post-launch support. Throughout development, I balanced fluctuating workloads, dedicating between 30-100% of my time while supporting myself. During the final six months, we collaborated with two interns in design and 3D art, an experience that was valuable for both them and me.
